About the College

The Faculty of Computers and Artificial Intelligence at Minya National University offers a comprehensive 4-year Bachelor's program designed to prepare students for the rapidly evolving field of AI.

Our program is taught primarily in English (with some humanities courses in Arabic) and follows the credit hour system with fall, spring, and optional summer semesters.

Upon successful completion, graduates receive a Bachelor of Science degree in Artificial Intelligence, equipping them with the knowledge and skills to excel in this cutting-edge field.

Our curriculum combines theoretical foundations with practical applications, ensuring students are well-prepared for both industry careers and advanced academic pursuits.

Academic Life

Diverse AI Fields

Students explore various AI domains including Machine Learning, Data Mining, Robotics, and Natural Language Processing through comprehensive coursework and research.

Hands-on Labs

Our state-of-the-art laboratories provide students with practical experience in implementing AI algorithms and working with real-world datasets.

Capstone Projects

In their final year, students work on graduation projects that solve real-world problems, demonstrating their accumulated knowledge and skills.

Curriculum Overview

AI Programming

Master Python and specialized AI libraries like TensorFlow and PyTorch for developing intelligent systems.

Deep Learning

Explore neural networks, backpropagation, and advanced architectures like CNNs and RNNs.

Data Science

Learn data preprocessing, visualization, and analysis techniques essential for AI applications.

Computer Vision

Study image processing, object detection, and pattern recognition algorithms.

Natural Language Processing

Understand how machines process and generate human language through AI techniques.

Robotics

Combine AI with mechanical systems to create intelligent robotic solutions.
